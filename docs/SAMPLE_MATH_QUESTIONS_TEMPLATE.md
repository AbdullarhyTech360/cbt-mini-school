## Mathematical Symbols You Can Use

### Basic Operations
- Addition: +
- Subtraction: −
- Multiplication: × or ·
- Division: ÷ or /
- Equals: =
- Not equals: ≠
- Approximately: ≈

### Comparison
- Less than: <
- Greater than: >
- Less than or equal: ≤
- Greater than or equal: ≥

### Greek Letters
- α (alpha), β (beta), γ (gamma), δ (delta)
- θ (theta), λ (lambda), μ (mu), π (pi)
- σ (sigma), φ (phi), ω (omega)

### Special Symbols
- Square root: √
- Infinity: ∞
- Plus-minus: ±
- Summation: ∑
- Integral: ∫
- Partial derivative: ∂
- Nabla: ∇

### Subscripts and Superscripts
- Subscripts: H₂O, CO₂, x₁, y₂
- Superscripts: x², x³, m², cm³

## LaTeX Examples

For more complex formulas, use LaTeX notation:

- Fractions: `$\frac{a}{b}$`
- Powers: `$x^{n}$`
- Roots: `$\sqrt{x}$` or `$\sqrt[n]{x}$`
- Integrals: `$\int_{a}^{b} f(x)dx$`
- Sums: `$\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i$`
- Limits: `$\lim_{x \to \infty} f(x)$`
- Matrices: `$\begin{matrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{matrix}$`
